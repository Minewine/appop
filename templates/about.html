About AppOp Demo

AppOp Demo is an AI-powered CV analysis and optimization tool designed to help job seekers improve their resumes and increase their chances of getting interviews.

Features

  • PDF CV/Resume Analysis
  • ATS (Applicant Tracking System) Compatibility Check
  • Job Description Matching Analysis
  • Keyword Optimization Suggestions
  • Content and Achievement Enhancement

How It Works

  1. Upload your CV/Resume (PDF format)
  2. Upload or paste a job description
  3. Our AI analyzes both documents
  4. Receive detailed feedback and suggestions
  5. Improve your CV based on the recommendations
